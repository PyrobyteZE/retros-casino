# Retro's Casino

A fun idle casino game with fake currency — click, gamble, and build your empire!

## Play Now

**[Play in your browser](https://pyrobyteze.github.io/retros-casino/)**

## Features

**Clicker**
- Tap to earn money with upgrades, auto-clickers, lucky clicks, and crits
- Rebirth system with VIP levels and multipliers

**Casino Games**
- Coin Flip — heads or tails with a lucky coin upgrade
- Slots — classic 3-reel slot machine with auto spin
- Crash — ride the multiplier, cash out before it crashes
- Blackjack — hit or stand against the dealer
- Plinko — drop balls through pegs for multipliers
- Roulette — full American roulette table with auto spin
- Horse Racing — pick a horse and bet on the odds
- Lottery — scratch cards with tiered prizes

**Properties**
- Buy and upgrade 7 legit businesses for passive income
- Hire managers to boost earnings
- Random events: Golden Hour, Tax Audit, Robberies, and more
- Defend against rival attacks

**Crime Empire** *(unlocks at VIP 1)*
- 7 illegal businesses from Bootleg DVDs to Arms Dealing
- Higher income than legit properties, but with risk
- Police raids — bribe cops or get shut down
- Risk scales with how many businesses you own

**Other**
- Loan Shark with interest and a card duel minigame
- Admin panel with cheats and rigging tools
- Saves automatically to local storage
- Works offline as a PWA
- Dark theme with multiple color options

## Android APK

Download the latest APK from [Releases](https://github.com/PyrobyteZE/retros-casino/releases).

Or build it yourself:

```bash
cd apk-build
ANDROID_HOME=~/android-sdk ./gradlew assembleDebug
```

The APK will be at `apk-build/app/build/outputs/apk/debug/app-debug.apk`
